Frequently Asked Questions

Responsive comes with all the features, functionalities and plugins you need to launch your WordPress Business website.

1. What is included in Responsive Pro bundle?

Responsive Pro bundle includes the Pro plugin that adds more features to the Responsive theme. It also includes access to 100+ Starter Website Templates & Responsive Elementor Addons with 50+ Elementor widgets.

2. What are Starter Site Templates?

Starter site templates are ready-to-customize WordPress websites. It is functional, not just design templates. It has the required plugins and copyright-free stock images. We believe this will save you substantial time to focus on your content and business. These theme templates can be imported using the template importer plugin.

3. How do I get support?

Active Responsive Pro customers can request support from My Account > Orders > Get Help. Free Responsive theme users can use the support forum in the official WordPress theme directory.

4. What is your refund policy?

We are 100% sure you all love Responsive. But we also understand it’s not for everyone. If for some reason you don’t want to continue using Responsive, get in touch with us within 30 days of your purchase. We’ll gladly refund your order amount.

5. Have another question?

Get in touch with us. We’ll be happy to help.
